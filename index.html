ABOUT ME

My name is Julia and I am a Dublin based designer, illustrator, coder, and all around creative-type person. Originally from the east coast of America, I graduated from the University of Delaware with a B.F.A. in Visual Communication. Now, two years later, I find myself at Trinity College Dublin working towards an M.Sc. in Interactive Digital Media. Drop me a line if you have any interesting projects!
